FAQ

How much are the delivery charges for an order on bigbasket, bb Express or Meat Express?

  1. For any order value below INR 250, a delivery fee of INR 50 will be applied. For any order value between INR 251 and INR 600, a delivery fee of INR 30 will be applied. All orders above INR 600 will not incur any delivery charge.

How much are the delivery charges for an order on bbnow?

  1. Delivery charges will be applied for bbnow orders based on various factors such as order value, time of day, weather etc.

If I forgot some items & added them as a separate order but it is getting delivered to me in the same slot as the others, will I get charged separately?

  1. In case the additional items order is scheduled in the same morning or evening slot as the one placed before, this is an add-on order with no additional delivery charge. It will have a separate bill but get delivered along with the previous order placed.
